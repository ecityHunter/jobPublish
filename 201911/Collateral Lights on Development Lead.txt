Job Purpose

The Collateral Technology team within Financial Markets IT is looking for a strong, hands-on candidate to fill the role of Lights On Development Lead. The Development Lead is expected to lead an Agile team of 8 or more engineers, dedicated to implementing solutions to build the strategic collateral management system to replace a legacy vendor platform. 

The Role Responsibilities
Scrum Master responsibility to run the daily scrums, and help facilitate the execution by members of the Agile team
Be part of an Agile team and contribute with hands on technical and functional development work to deliver on items in the product/project backlog
Track performance of the Agile team and help improve agility and velocity of the team
Contribute to functional and technical design and implementation of solutions
Contribute to implementation and enforcement of best practices related to standards and control of software code, testing and release management
Implement DevOps as part of the development practice of the Agile team to help automate code versioning, code build, testing, deployment from environment to environment, and release into production
